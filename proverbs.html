Proverbs

These swedish proverbs are great conversation starters. Or just maybe, they will motivate you to do that thing you always dreamed of doing?

  • I sense owls in the bog.
  • If there is room for the heart, there is also room for the butt.
  • No cow on the ice.
  • Now the boiled pork is pan-fried.
  • Sour, said the fox about the rowan-berries.
Bonus joke:

-Sheep sheep sheep?
-No, sheep sheep lamb.
